7. Health check-up and food planner app

This app checks your health day by day and suggests 
you proper meals that you should consume in order to remain healthy. 
It connects you to the numerous healthy-recipes that are provided by 
professional chef-bloggers. You can set your content to be provided as 
per your health situation, for e.g. if you are heart patient, you’ll be
recommended recipes made of ingredients that are heart-healthy. 
The app can partner with groceries to deliver healthy
items online directly from the app.
